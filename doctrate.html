P h . D . P r o g r a m m e s

Our Ph.D. program is a dedicated, full-time research initiative spanning an anticipated duration of 4 years, demanding a high level of dedication from participants. Students are required to reside in proximity to the University and fully engage in the program. Under the guidance of the postgraduate committee, doctoral candidates will craft a tailored study program focusing on a primary area of expertise alongside at least two secondary areas. Beyond coursework, the department conducts regular seminars where both students and faculty analyze and assess research within the field. To encourage constructive critique, students are encouraged to engage with a maximum of one research lab and actively participate in the group's research endeavors. The department specializes in research areas encompassing Intelligent Space and Machine Learning, Data Science/Big Data Analytics, Networking and Communications Engineering, and Cyber Security.
