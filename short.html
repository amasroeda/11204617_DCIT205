Our Short Course Programmes

Join our Short Course Program and unlock a world of opportunities in this technology-driven era. Acquiring new skills in ICT is crucial for propelling your career forward. Our program offers a dynamic platform to delve into the latest technological advancements, empowering you with the expertise needed to thrive in diverse professional landscapes. Gain invaluable skills that will serve as a springboard for your career growth, enabling you to harness the full potential of this fast-evolving digital age.

Our eight week intensive certificate course is open to senior high school graduates, tertiary students, school graduates, workers and the general public. Our instructors boast extensive years of hands-on experience, offering students a distinctive chance to learn from top professionals in the field. Upon completion of the program and successfully passing the prescribed examination, you'll receive a prestigious certificate of completion from the department. This certificate not only signifies your accomplishment but also validates your newfound knowledge and skills, providing a valuable asset for your career journey.
