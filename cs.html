The Department of Computer Science

The Computer Science department is lauded for its high-quality teaching, characterized by engaging lectures
and a hands-on approach that equips students with practical skills, preparing them effectively for real-world challenges in the field.
The faculty's expertise and commitment to student success contribute to the department's reputation for delivering top-notch education in computer science.

The department consistently organizes a range of programmes,
seminars, and events to enhance the academic and professional development of its members.
These initiatives include educational programs, skill-building workshops, and seminars led by experts, fostering valuable discussions and critical thinking
