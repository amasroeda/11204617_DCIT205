M P h i l / M S c P r o g r a m m e s

The University of Ghana's Department of Computer Science offers both a Master of Science (MSc.) in Computer Science and a Master of Philosophy (MPhil.) in Computer Science. The MSc. spans a year and is available as a weekend/evening program, featuring a capstone project. On the other hand, the MPhil. extends over two years as a full-time commitment, encompassing a year of structured coursework and a second year focused on research and a dissertation. These accelerated master’s programs cater to aspiring and current computer scientists seeking flexible study options, whether full-time or part-time. Participants in these programs receive comprehensive training in theoretical computer science and advanced technical skills, positioning them to harness technology as a transformative tool for addressing intricate challenges in global society. Upon completion, students gain practical experience in solving intricate, unstructured scientific problems, augmenting their expertise in subjects like Advanced Databases, Advanced Data Structures and Algorithms, and Networks.
