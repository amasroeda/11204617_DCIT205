Our Research Works

The Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana stands as an inspiring hub of innovation and exploration. Distinguished by its diverse array of research endeavors, it ignites curiosity and empowers students to pioneer groundbreaking projects. Encouraging an ethos of exploration, the department fosters an environment where students are not just learners but active contributors to technological advancements. From artificial intelligence to cybersecurity, each corner resonates with the hum of ideas and collaborative efforts. Professors serve as mentors, guiding students through the intricate maze of problem-solving and innovation. Emphasizing the practical application of knowledge, the department propels students to embark on projects that not only challenge the status quo but also serve as catalysts for societal betterment. This ethos cultivates a generation of forward-thinkers, poised to revolutionize the technological landscape and positively impact communities both near and far.

Some Past Research Works

Students at the University of Ghana have been actively involved in a multitude of research projects, including the creation of an Integrated Assets Management System for transportation, a Timetable Scheduling System, a Web-Based Hospital Management System, an Online Transport Information System for shuttle services, Web-Based Surveillance and Information Management Systems for security services, a Ticket Reservation System via SMS, a Web-Based Recruitment System for the Ghana Armed Forces, a Mobile Interface for the University's Students' Management Information System (MIS), a Customer Access Point for utility companies, an Information Management System for housing units, Network Monitoring and Management Software, Face Identification and Recognition System for student identification, a Web-Based Library Management System, an Employee Appraisal System, and the development of a FLOSS-based Distributed Application Warehouse
