E V E N T S

The Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana hosts a vibrant array of events throughout the academic year, fostering a dynamic environment for both students and faculty. From tech symposiums and hackathons to workshops and guest lectures by industry experts, these events offer invaluable opportunities for engagement and learning. Hackathons, in particular, ignite creativity and innovation, allowing students to collaborate, ideate, and prototype solutions to real-world problems within a constrained timeframe. Additionally, seminars and panel discussions featuring esteemed professionals offer insights into emerging trends and challenges within the tech sphere, enriching the academic experience and providing a platform for networking and knowledge exchange. These events not only complement the curriculum but also inspire a culture of exploration and continuous learning within the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana.
